① DCF on Earnings
Projects revenue for 10 years, ramps margins to your Year-10 target, proxies FCF at 85% of net profit. Discounts everything back at WACC and adds a terminal value. Verdict based on intrinsic value vs current price.
② Reverse Growth Check
Works backwards from today's enterprise value (EV) to find the revenue CAGR it prices in. Using EV instead of market cap avoids flagging debt-heavy companies as cheap when they are actually leveraged.
③ Historical EV/Revenue
Compares current EV/Revenue to the stock's own 5-year average. EV/Revenue is more robust than P/S for capital-intensive businesses. Skipped if fewer than 3 years of data.
④ Sector P/E Fair Value
Multiplies the company's EPS by the sector's typical P/E multiple to get a simple fair value estimate. Most useful as a sanity check against DCF — if both agree, the signal is stronger.
